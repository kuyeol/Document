## For the Instructor

**Instructor's Resource CD** (ISBN 0-87893-750-1)

This expanded resource includes all the figures and tables from the textbook in JPEG format, reformatted and relabeled for optimal readability.
Also included are ready-to-use PowerPoint® presentations of all figures and tables.
In addition, new for the Third Edition, the Instructor's Resource CD includes a set of short-answer study questions for each chapter in Microsoft® Word® format.

**Overhead Transparencies** (ISBN 0-87893-751-X)

This set includes 100 illustrations (approximately 150 transparencies), selected from throughout the textbook for teaching purposes.
These are relabeled and optimized for projection in classrooms.
